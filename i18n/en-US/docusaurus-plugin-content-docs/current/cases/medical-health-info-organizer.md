# Case: Medical Health Info Organizer

## Audience

People preparing appointment materials for themselves or family members.

## Problems Solved

Scattered symptom records, forgotten questions, and difficult-to-read reports.

## Vibe Coding Workflow

Record facts, ask AI to organize a timeline and question list, then check health knowledge against authoritative sources.

## Recommended Tools

Spreadsheets, notes, OCR, MedlinePlus, and CDC.

## Example Prompt

```text
Only organize facts; do not diagnose. Output symptom timeline, medication record, test summary, and questions for the clinician.
```

## Reference Project

Create a family health log with date, symptom, medication, report, and clinician feedback.

## Limitations and Risks

Emergency symptoms should go directly to medical services instead of waiting for AI analysis.

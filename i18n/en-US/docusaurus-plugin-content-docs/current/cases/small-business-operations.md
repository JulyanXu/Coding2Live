# Case: Small Business Operations

## Audience

Small teams that want low-cost improvements to orders, customer service, inventory, and daily reports.

## Problems Solved

Oral processes, repeated replies, and manual summaries.

## Vibe Coding Workflow

Break repeated actions into trigger, input, process, and output. Ask AI for SOPs, spreadsheet fields, and automation sketches.

## Recommended Tools

Google Workspace, Notion, and Zapier.

## Example Prompt

```text
Turn this order-handling flow into an SOP and automation plan. Separate what we can do now from what should be custom-built later.
```

## Reference Project

Form intake, spreadsheet summary, automated reminder, and daily digest.

## Limitations and Risks

Validate process stability before building a formal system.

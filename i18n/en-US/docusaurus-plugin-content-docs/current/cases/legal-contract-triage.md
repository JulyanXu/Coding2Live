# Case: Legal and Contract Triage

## Audience

Teams that need to organize contract information before legal review.

## Problems Solved

Scattered clauses, unclear internal questions, and inefficient counsel communication.

## Vibe Coding Workflow

Redact sensitive data, ask AI only for summary, classification, and questions, then send the result to legal staff for judgment.

## Recommended Tools

General AI assistant, document diff tool, and a contract triage sheet.

## Example Prompt

```text
Only perform contract triage; do not provide legal advice. Output summary, clause table, risk questions, and required human checks.
```

## Reference Project

Track every contract with clause, risk, owner, and counsel-question columns.

## Limitations and Risks

AI cannot replace counsel; sensitive contracts should not be uploaded to non-compliant services.

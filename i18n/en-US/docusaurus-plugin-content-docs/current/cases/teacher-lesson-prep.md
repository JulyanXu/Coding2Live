# Case: Teacher Lesson Prep

## Audience

Teachers who need to prepare lessons, exercises, quizzes, and handouts quickly.

## Problems Solved

Limited prep time, few tiered exercises, and unclear activity instructions.

## Vibe Coding Workflow

Provide objectives and student profile, ask AI for lesson flow, activities, exercises, and rubrics, then review manually.

## Recommended Tools

Google AI education resources, Moodle, and a general AI assistant.

## Example Prompt

```text
Design a 45-minute lesson for this topic with objectives, flow, activities, exercises, homework, and teacher verification points.
```

## Reference Project

Import mature exercises into Moodle quizzes and build a local question bank.

## Limitations and Risks

Verify facts, curriculum alignment, and student privacy requirements.

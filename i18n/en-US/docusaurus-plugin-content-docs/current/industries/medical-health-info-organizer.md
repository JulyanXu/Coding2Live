# Medical Health Info Organizer

## Audience

People who need to organize symptom timelines, medication records, test results, and appointment questions for themselves or family members.

## Problems Solved

- Turn scattered symptoms, dates, medications, and events into a timeline.
- Generate questions to ask a clinician.
- Summarize authoritative health information into personal reading notes.

## Vibe Coding Workflow

1. Record facts: time, symptoms, temperature, medication, dose, and test results.
2. Ask AI to create a timeline and clinician question list.
3. Use authoritative sources such as MedlinePlus, CDC, and WHO for health knowledge.
4. Treat AI output as communication material, not diagnosis.
5. Update the record after the visit and keep the clinician's original advice.

## Recommended Tools

- Spreadsheets or notes for symptom and test records.
- OCR tools for turning reports into searchable text.
- Authoritative health sites for basic fact checks.

## Example Prompt

```text
Organize these health records for a medical appointment:
1. Only organize facts and questions; do not diagnose.
2. Output a symptom timeline, medications, and test-result summary.
3. Generate 8 questions for the clinician.
4. Mark missing information that may affect judgment.
5. Point me to MedlinePlus, CDC, or WHO for health knowledge checks.
```

## Reference Project

Create a shared family health log with date, symptom, medication, report, photo, and clinician feedback fields.

## Limitations and Risks

Health advice is high risk. AI may misread symptoms or reports. Emergency symptoms should be handled through local emergency or medical services.

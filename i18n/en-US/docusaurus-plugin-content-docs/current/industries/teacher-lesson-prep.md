# Teacher Lesson Prep

## Audience

Teachers or trainers who need to prepare lesson outlines, exercises, quizzes, handouts, and feedback materials quickly.

## Problems Solved

- Turn standards, textbook passages, and student profiles into executable lesson plans.
- Generate tiered exercises for students at different levels.
- Package activities, quizzes, and assignments as reusable templates.

## Vibe Coding Workflow

1. Provide learning objectives, grade level, class length, and student profile.
2. Ask AI to draft the lesson flow, board structure, exercises, and evaluation criteria.
3. Use scripts or spreadsheets to batch-generate question banks, answers, and handouts.
4. Review facts, difficulty, values, and local curriculum alignment manually.
5. Save the mature template for future lessons.

## Recommended Tools

- Google AI education resources for common teaching use cases.
- GitHub Copilot or a general coding assistant for question banks and classroom utilities.
- Moodle for courses, quizzes, and assignment modules.

## Example Prompt

```text
Act as a middle-school science teacher. Design a 45-minute lesson on energy conservation:
1. Students understand speed and mass but are new to energy transfer.
2. Output objectives, class flow, 2 demos, 8 tiered exercises, and homework.
3. For each activity, include materials, timing, and likely misconceptions.
4. End with facts the teacher must verify manually.
```

## Reference Project

Start with a Moodle course structure, import AI-generated exercises into quiz modules, and gradually build a school-specific question bank.

## Limitations and Risks

AI may produce incorrect facts or activities that do not match local standards. Do not upload personally identifiable student data to services that are not approved for that use.
